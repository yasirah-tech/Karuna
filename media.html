KARUNA

Karuna is a community for people who became victims of domestic violence or abuse. This website is promoting a safe space for the victims to find information, inspire from other victims stories and find help to save themselves from an abusive environment.

The Creation Of A Movement

#MaybeHeDoesntHitYou was a movement that trended on twitter a week ago where people would share their stories on how domestic abuse can come in forms other than physical. Women from all across the internet would share stories not about physical but mental and psychological abuse that they had to endure in their relationships. That was the premise of the movement. Originally this movement was created in 2016 but recently has come back to the surface of the internet to educate more people on the problem. You can learn more on the original movement and its starting point here.

Mental manipulation.

In this tweet, an pakistanian women named Kanwhal Ahmed fell prey to mental manipulation, making her believe that her partner was all she needed and couldn't see her friends and family because he led her to believe that she didn't need them. She now dedicates her time to creating a safe space to educate and discuss women's personal problems so they can find a solution. You can find her help here.

Dehumanization.

Becca was another woman that suffered another example of psychological abuse, which was dehumanization. She felt as if she was degraded to his sex toy, because that was the only way to get affection from her partner which should never be the case in relationships, leading her to have a twisted state of mind and to the boyfriend, nothing but a tool.

Ridiculous expectations and standards

This tweet was from a women who suffered complete control to her partner.However what it boils down to is a sense of entitlement and setting the standard for what they want their partner to do. The partner set strict standards for the counterpart and believes that they are entitled to those standards, when actually, that is not the case, and they are not keeping their relationship healthy, but rather ,making their partner feel like they have no freedom.
