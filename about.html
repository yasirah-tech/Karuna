Gurupriyan Jayakaran

My name is Gurupriyan Jayakaran. Domestic violence in a family destabilizes the foundation of the family in which it was built and families create the future of the country and if this is what the new generation is built on, the future of the country will be in turmoil. When people are informed about the negative effects and consequences of such a relationship and given enough support to eventually help them break the violence. This is the objective of our project.

Yasirah Boyce

My name is Yasirah Boyce and I am a twenty-three year old Brooklyn native. I took part in creating this website with my team to contribute to spreading awareness on an issue that I have witnessed first-hand, but at a time where I did not have the resources to support this person. Moving forward, I will collaborate with my team to create a community to support individuals in domestically violent relationships and abusive situations.
